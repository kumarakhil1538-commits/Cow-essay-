Essay on a Normal Cow

Introduction

A cow is a domestic animal. It is a very useful animal for humans. It gives us milk which is very healthy. Many people in villages keep cows in their homes.

Importance of Cow

The cow gives us milk, curd, butter, and ghee. Farmers use cow dung as manure in fields. Cow dung is also used to make fuel.

Conclusion

The cow is a gentle and helpful animal. We should take care of cows and treat them kindly.
